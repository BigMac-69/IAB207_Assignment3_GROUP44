Experience the best of Vietnamese cuisine

Event Description:
Discover the best of Vietnamese Banh Mi culture at our upcoming event! Join us for a day of excellent cuisine, fascinating activities, and lively cultural performances. Enjoy a wide range of banh mi delicacies, from traditional pork and pâté to creative vegetarian options, all made by local vendors and renowned chefs. View live demonstrations of banh mi preparation and learn the art of balancing flavors and textures that make this iconic sandwich so popular. Beyond banh mi, try additional Vietnamese street food favorites and drink aromatic Vietnamese coffee and tea. Immerse yourself in Vietnamese culture with traditional music and dance performances, and informative exhibits that highlight the country's rich past. Bring the whole family for fun activities like easy cookery workshops and Vietnamese-inspired face painting. Whether you're a foodie, a culture buff, or simply seeking for a one-of-a-kind day out, our Banh Mi Festival guarantees a memorable experience that will transfer your senses directly to the streets of Vietnam. Don't pass up this opportunity to appreciate the bright tastes and friendly hospitality of Vietnamese cuisine!
Event Highlights:
  • Live Banh Mi Making Process Demonstrations
  • Traditional Vietnamese Music Performances
  • Varieties Banh Mi Tasting Events
  • Meet and Greet with Top Vietnamese Chefs
